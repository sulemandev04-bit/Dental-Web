Our Story

Redefining the Dental Experience since 2010

BrightSmile started with a simple goal: to remove the fear from dentistry. Our founder, Dr. Sarah Jenkins, envisioned a clinic where advanced technology meets a spa-like environment.

Today, we serve over 5,000 patients annually, offering everything from routine cleanings to complex oral surgeries, all performed with the same gentle care we'd give our own family.
